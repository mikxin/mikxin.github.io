Chefs

Image: Ambro / FreeDigitalPhotos.net

Pastry chef Susan Baker and her sister, Amy Baker, are the co-owners of Pastry Lover Bakery in Madison, Wisconsin. They were both trained at the UW-Madison Pastry School. Susan and Amy traveled, trained, and cooked in France and upon their return, opened Pastry Lover Bakery in Madison, Wisconsin.

Susan was named Pastry Chef of the Year in Madison in 2010. Both of them were nominated for James Beard Award for Outstanding Pastry Chefs in 2009 and 2010, and won the award in 2011.
