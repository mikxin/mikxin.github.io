Malacca

Cendol (Shaved ice with red beans and green jelly)

So this is cendol! Malaysia's weather is usually hot throughout the year so, cendol is really a great thirst quencher on hot afternoons. After ordering a cendol for yourself, you talked to the stall owner and he told you that the A'Famosa Fortress is a must-see and also, there will be a mid-autumn festival celebration here at the street tonight. Would you want to go to the A'Famosa now? Or you would like to try the other food in this hawker center?

  • Go A'Famosa
  • No, go straight to the festival site
  • Try other food!

The green jelly is made from rice flour and gets its color from Pandan leaves
