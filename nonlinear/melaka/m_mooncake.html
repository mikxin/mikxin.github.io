Mid-Autumn Festival

Although Malaysia does not have four seasons, Chinese in Malaysia celebrate Mid-Autumn Festival aka Mooncake Festival. It is celebrated on the day of full moon and there are many different versions of the origin of this festival. Family members usually gather at night and enjoy playing lanterns or eating mooncake. You sat down at a table and munch on the mooncake while admiring the colorful lanterns hanging across the street. It's about time to head back to hotel, you decided to do this the next morning:

  • Fly back home
  • Go visit other states

Other food eaten during this festival include taro and water caltrop
