Malacca

Wooden Clogs

Wooden clogs used to be a kind of cheap footwear worn by Malaccans. Nowadays, people seldom wear these slippers but start making more beautiful wooden clogs as a form of craft. After buying a pair of the clogs, you talked to the stall owner and she told you that you must go check out the A'Famosa Fortress and there will be a mid-autumn festival celebration here at the street tonight. Would you want to go to the A'Famosa fortress now?

  • Yes!
  • No, go straight to the festival site

Wooden clogs are believed to be a form of influence from the Dutch.
