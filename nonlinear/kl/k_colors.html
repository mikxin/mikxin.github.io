Colors of Malaysia

Colors of Malaysia is a celebration of the multicultural of the people in Malaysia. It is one of the biggest event in Malaysia that attracts many tourists from all over the world. Performers showcase the different costumes of different ethnics and perform traditional dances. It is a great chance for Malaysians to strengthen the bonds among the different ethics. The main ethnics in Malaysia are Malay, Chinese, Indian, Iban and Kadazan
