Welcome to Kuala Lumpur

the Capital of Malaysia!

After the plane landed at the Kuala Lumpur International Airport (KLIA), you took a cab to the Kuala Lumpur PETRONAS Twin Tower. This twin structure is 451.9 meters in height and consists of 88 stories. It is inspired by Malaysia Forth Prime Minister, Tun Dr Mahathir Mohamad's vision for Malaysia to be a global player (credits). You take an elevator to the skybridge, which connects the two towers at level 41 and see the breathtaking scenery of the city of Kuala Lumpur. After that, your trip planner displays choices of next destination for you:

  • KL Tower
  • Batu Caves

PETRONAS is a Malaysian oil and gas company that was founded on August 17, 1974
