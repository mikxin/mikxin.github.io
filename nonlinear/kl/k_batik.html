Kuala Lumpur International Batik (KLIB) Convention and Exhibition

Batik is a kind of fabric art created using wax resist dyeing technique. Although it was said to originated from Java, Indonesia, batik is found in everyday life of Malaysians. Batik can be made into clothes for both male and female and is often worn by Malaysian in formal events.
