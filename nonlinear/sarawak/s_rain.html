Rainforest World Music Festival

Rainforest World Music Festival is an event held annually in Sarawak that lasts 3 days. Music lovers from all around the world gather here and share their passion for music. The activities in the course of these 3 days include stage performance traditional local music as well as contemporary music from other parts of the world. There are also workshops and exhibition during the day. After you enjoyed the live performance by fantastic music lovers, it's about time to head back to hotel.   • Fly back home
  • Go visit other states
