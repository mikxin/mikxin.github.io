Orang Utan

You take a cab to the Semenggoh Wildlife Centre. It is the home of many wildlife as well as a center for research for wildlife rehabilitation. This center is especially well known as the center for studying the endangered species orang utan. The orang utan is found in the rainforests of Malaysian Borneo, Indonesian Borneo and North Sumatra.

After visiting the National Park, your trip adviser suggests two more places for you:

  • Rainforest World Music Festival
  • Arts and crafts bazaar

The scientific name of Borneon orang utan is Pongo Pygmaeus
